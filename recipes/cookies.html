Cookies

Description

Cookies are a type of confectionery made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients that are baked or fried.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter or margarine
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Roll the dough into small balls.
  3. Place the cookies on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake the cookies.
