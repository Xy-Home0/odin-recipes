Pizza

Description

Our pizza is made with the finest ingredients and cooked to perfection in our brick ov en. It's a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for sharing with friends and family. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of active dry yeast
  • 5 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 (14 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Assorted toppings (pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes, or until foamy.
  3. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough, and divide into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a 12-inch circle.
  7. Place the dough on a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  8. In a small bowl, combine the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Spread the sauce over the dough.
  9. Top with the mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and assorted toppings.
  10. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
